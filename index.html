About me

I'm Joseph Mackle and I'm a Game Design Student

Since September of 2019, I have been attending Abertay University to study Game Design and Production, this is a 4 year course. Before coming to Abertay University I attended Southern Regional College where I studied Games, Animation and VFX for 2 years, completing it in June of 2019.
