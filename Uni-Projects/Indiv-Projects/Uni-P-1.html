User Interfaces and Interaction Design

Project Brief

"Students are required to complete a portfolio of research and practice related to user interface and interaction design. This portfolio should evidence the student’s developed knowledge and practice of the subject matter in accordance to the context of the brief. Students will be graded based solely on the evidence provided within the portfolio"


Portfolio of Practice | 100% Weighting


Creative Brief A: Augmented Reality (AR)

“Design an AR game for mobile devices that enhances a curated exhibit.”


Learning Outcomes:

By the end of this module the student should be able to:


  1. Demonstrate an understanding of UI design practices sensibilities, and theories;
  2. Research audiences and platforms to inform UX design that supports effective communication, accessibility, and interaction;
  3. Iteratively develop and test UI prototypes informed by knowledge and understanding of interaction design
